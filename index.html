About Us

About PuraLawAI

PuraLawAI is an AI-powered legal assistance platform developed in Assam, India, with a vision to make quality legal guidance accessible to every Indian — regardless of location, language, or economic background.

We are currently in active development and internal testing. PuraLawAI is built on top of advanced large language models and is designed specifically for the Indian legal system, incorporating BNS 2023, BNSS 2023, BSA 2023 and other landmark legislation alongside established law.

Our platform serves advocates, judges, police officers and common citizens across India. We want to integrate with Bhashini to bring voice-enabled legal assistance in 36+ Indian languages.

PuraLawAI is a prototype product of Kaushik Sarma, developer and founder, based in Nalbari, Assam — 781353.
